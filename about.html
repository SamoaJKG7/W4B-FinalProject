A Night In?

As early sunday, the count down to the weekend begins. We day dream about kicking back and relaxing or taking a night on the town and knockig down as many bars as possible. The year of 2020 changed that for a lot of us. Covid restrictions took a lot of our weekend fantasies hostage and has kept a stronghold on them for a couple years now, with mask requiremnts and vaccination cards. So what do you do when you cant go to the bar? You brign the bar to you! Sip was created with your weekend fantasies and convience in mind. Whether your throwing a party, hosting a few friends for a relaxing evening or just want to sip leisurely and enjoy your favorite pasttime. Regardless of the occassion, Sip Craft Cocktails is at your service.
